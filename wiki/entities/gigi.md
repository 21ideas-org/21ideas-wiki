# Gigi (dergigi)

*Tags: entity, person, Bitcoin-philosopher, author*

---

## Who He Is

Gigi (pen name; Twitter: @dergigi) is a Bitcoin author, philosopher, and developer. He is one of the most influential Bitcoin thinkers on the philosophical and sociological aspects of Bitcoin. He runs dergigi.com and has contributed to 21ideas.org as both author and translator.

---

## Key Works in This Library

**"21 Ways"** (*21 способов*) — Book: 21 short essays, each presenting a different frame for understanding Bitcoin. Philosophical, technical, economic, sociological. See [[books/21-ways]].

**"Proof of Life"** — Article arguing Bitcoin meets the criteria for a living organism: metabolism (energy consumption via [[concepts/proof-of-work|PoW]]), homeostasis ([[concepts/mining|difficulty adjustment]]), reproduction (node propagation), evolution (protocol upgrades via consensus). Source: `raw/Theory/philosophy/proof-of-life.md`

**"Bitcoin is the Meme"** — Bitcoin is unique among memes: it creates real-world effects as it spreads. Each holder becomes an advocate; advocacy drives adoption; adoption drives value; value creates more advocates. Bitcoin is a self-replicating monetary organism. Source: `raw/Theory/philosophy/bitcoin-meme.md`

**"Inalienable Property Rights"** — Property rights depend on enforcement mechanisms. Physical property depends on state enforcement. Bitcoin's cryptographic property rights are inalienable — neither the state nor any third party can take them from you if you control the keys. Source: `raw/Theory/philosophy/inalienable-property-rights.md`

**"Freedom of Value"** — The value-for-value model: creators paid directly by audience via [[concepts/lightning-network|Lightning]] micropayments without platform intermediaries. Bitcoin + Lightning + Nostr as infrastructure for a censorship-resistant creator economy. Source: `raw/Theory/philosophy/freedom-of-value.md`

**"Bitcoin, not Crypto"** (*crypto-bro.md*) — Strong argument against altcoins. Bitcoin is not blockchain, not DeFi, not Web3, not CBDC, not yield farming. These distinctions matter. Source: `raw/Theory/economics/not-shitcoin.md`

**"What I Learned From Bitcoin"** — 3-part series on philosophical, economic, and technical lessons from deep Bitcoin study. Source: `raw/Theory/philosophy/what-i-learned-from-bitcoin/`

## Core Philosophical Themes

1. **Bitcoin as living organism**: applies biological concepts directly to the protocol
2. **Inalienable cryptographic property**: highest form of property rights in history
3. **Bitcoin as truth machine**: the blockchain produces objective, unforgeable records
4. **Value-for-value**: Lightning as infrastructure for creator sovereignty
5. **Bitcoin as idea**: once understood, Bitcoin cannot be "uninvented" — it is a permanent addition to human knowledge
