## Why support matters

21wiki is a free, open Bitcoin education resource. It is built and maintained by [Tony](https://njump.me/npub10awzknjg5r5lajnr53438ndcyjylgqsrnrtq5grs495v42qc6awsj45ys7) and a small group of contributors as part of the [21ideas](https://21ideas.org) project.

If the wiki has been useful to you, consider sending a few sats. It helps cover the time spent maintaining sources, improving pages, and keeping the infrastructure running.

## Other ways to help

Not ready to zap? You can still contribute:

- **Suggest a missing topic** — open a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/21ideas-org/21ideas-wiki/issues/new/choose)
- **Fix an error** — same link, use the "Report an error" template
- **Add content** — see the [Contribute](./contribute) page for how to add new wiki pages with AI assistance
- **Star the repo** — [21ideas-wiki on GitHub](https://github.com/21ideas-org/21ideas-wiki) — it helps the project get discovered
